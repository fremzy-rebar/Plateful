About content & copyright
Plateful is a personal aggregator — it helps you collect and organize links to recipes published publicly on the web.
  • Recipes stay on their original websites
  • Plateful stores links, not full content
  • No recipe text is copied or redistributed
  • Your own notes & photos are yours alone
I understand that Plateful collects links to publicly available recipes and does not reproduce copyrighted content.
